How to Play

Candy Rain is an easy-to-learn but challenging-to-master puzzle game. Follow these simple steps to start your sweet adventure!

1

Swap Candies

Switch any two adjacent candies to create a match of 3 or more identical candies in a row or column.

2

Complete Objectives

Check the cookie panel on the left to see your current level goals and remaining moves.

3

Create Power-Ups

Match 4 or 5 candies to create special bombs that clear multiple candies with a single move.

4

Earn Stars

Score high points to earn stars and unlock treasure chests on the level map.
